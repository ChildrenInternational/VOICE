Hyper-personalization — Terms of Use

Version 1.0 · You must accept these terms once before using the style fingerprint feature.

1. Your voice, or voices you're authorized to speak for. The hyper-personalization feature builds a writing voice from the answers and writing samples you provide. You may only create personalized voices that represent you, or a person or role you have been explicitly authorized to write on behalf of.

2. No impersonation. You must not use this feature to create content in the voice of another person — a colleague, an executive, a public figure, anyone — without their explicit authorization. Doing so is a misuse of this tool, regardless of intent.

3. Attribution and accountability. Voices you create are attributed to your signed-in account. You remain responsible for content produced with your personalized voices, exactly as if you had written it yourself.

4. Committee oversight. The AI Advancement Committee may review, restrict, or remove personalized voices and may revoke access to this feature at any time.
